Minimal, notebook-driven repository for learning **Speech & Language Processing (NLP + Speech)** through hands-on experiments.


## Scope
- Text preprocessing & language models  
- Word embeddings & sequence models  
- Transformers & NLP tasks  
- Speech fundamentals & feature extraction (MFCC, spectrograms)

## Pinned Task Sources
- **ex1.pdf — Electronics Review Mining with VADER + NER**  
	Source: [Kaggle — Consumer Reviews of Amazon Products](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/datafiniti/consumer-reviews-of-amazonproducts)
- **ex2.pdf — BOW/TF-IDF vs LDA Topic Models for Classification**  
	Source: [scikit-learn — 20 Newsgroups Dataset](https://scikit-learn.org/stable/datasets/real_world.html#the-20-newsgroups-text-dataset)
- **ex3.pdf — Embedding-Based Sentiment Classification (Product Reviews)**  
	Source: [NLTK — product_reviews_1 Corpus](https://www.nltk.org/howto/corpus.html)
- **ex4.pdf — Skip-Gram Word2Vec from Scratch**  
	Source: [Text8 Corpus (Wikipedia subset)](https://huggingface.co/datasets/text8)
- **ex5.pdf — CNN Sentiment Classifier for Movie Reviews**  
	Source: [Keras — IMDB Dataset](https://keras.io/api/datasets/imdb/)
- **ex6.pdf — BiLSTM for Named Entity Recognition**  
	Source: [CoNLL-2003 Dataset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/conll2003)
- **ex7.pdf — Abstractive Summarization (Seq2Seq + Attention)**  
	Source: [CNN/DailyMail Dataset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/cnn_dailymail)
